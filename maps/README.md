# Maps

This directory contains representative cartographic outputs generated from the geospatial analysis workflows developed for the Tehran Megaregion study.

The maps illustrate key environmental and urban indicators derived from Google Earth Engine datasets and spatial processing techniques.

## Built-up Areas (2021)

![Built-up Areas](builtup_2021.jpg)

Description:

* Spatial distribution of built-up surfaces in 2021.
* Red pixels indicate built-up land cover.
* Derived from GLC_FCS30D Global Land Cover data.

---

## Built-up Area Change (2009–2021)

![Built-up Change](builtup_change_2009_2021.jpg)

Description:

* Spatial intensity of urban expansion between 2009 and 2021.
* Higher values represent stronger increases in built-up land cover.

---

## Night-Time Lights

![Night Lights](nightlights_2021.jpg)

Description:

* Spatial distribution of night-time light intensity.
* Used as a proxy for urbanization and economic activity.
* Derived from NOAA VIIRS observations.

---

## Air Pollution Change (2018–2021)

![Air Pollution Change](air_pollution_change_2018_2021.jpg)

Description:

* Combined change pattern of NO₂, PM₂.₅ and PM₁₀ concentrations.
